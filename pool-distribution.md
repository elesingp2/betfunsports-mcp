Pool distribution

General provision
Winnings in Betfunsports are paid out from the prize pool, which is formed by the players' bets.
The fee for bet is charged separately from the bet into the prize Fund.
For each coupon, four pools are formed on four betting tables:
Wooden
Bronze
Silver
Gold
The bet plays only in one pool on the table where it was made.
The rules of distribution of winnings are the same for all types of coupons and betting tables.
The distribution of the prize fund is carried out in two stages:
1st stage - the determination of winning bets.
2nd stage - calculation of winnings for each bet.

Determination of winning bets
The determination of winning bets is carried out in two stages:
1st stage - ranking of bets.
2nd stage - selection of winning bets.

Ranking of bets
After prediction accuracy evaluation, all bets are ranked according to the following criteria:
Criterion No 1. Evaluation of the accuracy of the prediction.
The bets are sorted in descending order of points.
Criterion No 2. Bet value.
If points for accuracy are equal the bets are sorted in the descending order of bet size.
Criterion No 3. Time of the bet.
When bet sizes are equal, the bets are ranked from earlier to later.
A bet made earlier a greater priority for a winning than a bet made later if they have equal points for accuracy and equal bet sizes.

Selection of winning bets
After the bets are ranked, the winning bets are determined based on the following rules:
Rule No 1. Bets with accuracy of 100 points always win.
Rule No 2. The number of winning bets is:
50% of the total number of bets if the number of bets is even an number.
50% of the total number of bets minus one if the number of bets is add.
Rule No 1 has priority over Rule No 2.

Calculation winnings
After the winning bets are determined, the winnings are calculated according to the following rules:
Rule No 1. The prize pool is fully distributed.
Rule No 2. The size of the winnings is proportional to the forecast accuracy of the points.
Rule No 3. The size of the winnings is proportional to the value of the bet.
Rule No 4. The minimum winning ratio is 1.3 (excluding commission) except for the case specified in Rule No 5.
Rule No 5. In case that more than 50% of the bets get 100 points for accuracy, the winnings are distributed thern, in proportion to bet size.

Bet and commission fees refund
Bets and commission fees are refunded in the following cases:
Only one bet was made in the pool.
All bets received 100 points for accuracy.
The coupon was cancelled in connection with the cancellation of the event.
The pool draw was cancelled as a fraud was detected.
