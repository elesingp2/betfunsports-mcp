How To Bet

In Betfunsports, betters compete for the prize pool among themselves.
The pool is formed through the players' bets.
Bets, depending on the size, are made on 4 tables:
A Wooden table for betting in virtual currency BFS (free betting);
A bronze table for betting from 1 to 5 euros
A Silver table for betting from 10 to 50 euros
A Gold table for betting from 100 to 500 euros
A bet is made using a coupon.
The betting process consists of three stages:

To select a coupon
To make a forecast
To put money into the pool
Thus, for each coupon, 4 pools are formed.
Commission fee for the bet is charged separately, not from the prize pool.

General information about a coupon
The coupon consists of three blocks:
The coupon's header contains general information on the coupon and serves to search the coupon.
The coupon's core contains one or more events offered for betting and serves for prediction.
The lower part of the coupon contains information about betting tables, including information about placed bets and serves to select the size of the stake.

Coupons for football
Match outcome 
This coupon is similar to the traditional bookmaker's bet on the outcome (head-to-head):
1 - home win
X - draw
2 – away win
For play-off matches, only the main time of the match is taken into account (90 min + stoppage time).
Match Winner
This coupon applies only to playoff matches.
It is necessary to choose the winner of the match taking into account the possible additional time and a series of post-match penalties:
1 - home win
2 – away win
Play-off match outcome
This coupon applies only to playoff matches and it is an integration of two coupons:
Match outcome and Match Winner.
First, a prediction for the outcome of the main time of the match is made:
1 - home win
X - draw
2 – away win
If the main time of the match is a draw then you need to make a prediction on the match winner on the basis of overtime or a penalty shootout.
Outcome of second match and series winner
This coupon is for the second match in the playoff series of two matches.
The result of the first match is known and shown in the coupon.
You need to predict the outcome of the second match:
1 - home win
X - draw
2 – away win
If it is impossible to clearly determine the winner of the series taking into account the outcome of the first match then you need to make an additional prediction of the winner of the series.
Outcomes of 2 matches and winner of the play-off series
This coupon is for prediction on the outcomes of the two matches and the playoff series winner.
You need to predict the outcome of two playoff series matches.
In the first match:
1 - home win
X - draw
2 – away win
In the second match:
1- away win (home of the first match)
X - draw
2 – home win (away of the first match)
If the winner of the series is not obvious when the outcomes of two matches are known then you need to additionally specify the winner of the series.
Goal difference
This coupon is for prediction on the difference between the scored and missed goals in the match (the main time of the match for playoff matches).
You need to choose one of seven options:

≥3 - a home win with a difference of 3 or more goals
2 – a home win with a difference of 2 goals
1 - home win with 1 goal differences
Draws
1 - away win with 1 goal differences
2 – away win with 2 goals differences
≥3 - away win with a difference of 3 or more goalss
