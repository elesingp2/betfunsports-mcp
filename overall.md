Betfunsports is a new betting system. All bets are added to a prize pool to be fully distributed among the players.
The rules are that 50% of the bets win.
Bets (forecasts) get accuracy points from 0 to 100.
Winnings are proportional to accuracy points and bet size.
A bet is made via a coupon. For each coupon, there are 3 pools depending on the bet size: Bronze, Silver and Gold.
Free betting in virtual currency BFS is available after a simplified registration.
